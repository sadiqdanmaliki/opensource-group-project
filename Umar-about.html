About This Project

This Student Result Management System is a final year project developed by Group J at Al-Qalam University Katsina. Our goal is to provide a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly platform that simplifies the process of recording and calculating student academic results.

.

>As a team of Software Engineering students, we have leveraged modern web technologies to build a system that ensures data accuracy, promotes transparency, and streamlines result management for both students and faculty members. This project demonstrates our dedication to creating practical solutions for real-world academic challenges/p>
